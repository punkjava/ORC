Hii Students,<br>
	According to the revised method for concession approval the following steps must be followed:<br>
	1.Make sure that you have a Adhar Card to avail to the facilities.The previously used id proof is not valid now.<br>
	2.The details entered during the admission should match with the form details else your form wont be accepted.<br>
	3.Girls can apply for monthly concession which is free for them.<br>
	4.Boys can apply monthly,quarterly and avail the concession.<br>
	5.Enter the proper ticket number else your request wont be accepted.<br>
	6.In the status you can view your status information wherein you will get a unique id using which you can collect the form from 
	  the assigned authority.<br>
	7.History can be viewed.<br>
